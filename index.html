One Piece Devil Fruits

What Are Devil Fruits?

Devil Fruits are from the show One Piece, they have the ability to grant a unique power according to each type of fruit consumed. But, if a person eats a Devil fruit, while they gain the ability of the power that the type of Devil Fruit holds, they also lose the ability to swim. This is a big deal, due to the One Piece world being mostly consisting of a large body of water compared to the land. Which is why they were given the name “Devil fruits”, because some consider their use a curse that is only needed for an individuals desire while the ocean sea rejects them. Devil Fruits are the main source of power for expert fighters like Marines, Yonko, and pirates in One Piece since they quickly give special, frequently devastating superpowers on the user.

A Devil Fruit power can only be possessed by one person at a time, but once the old user has passed way, it will reappear again in the world after their death. Additionally, there is a rule on how there can only be one user of a Devil Fruit power and can nt obtain more than one power. Devil fruits fall into three main categories: the most frequently found, Paramecia, which grants a wide range of superhuman abilities. Zoan, which bestows the ability to change into animals. And Logia, the rarest and most potent, which offers the ability to change into a natural element. Each fruit has a distinct appearance that makes identification and documentation available.

Devil Fruit Types

Paramecia
Paramecia Devil Fruits are the most common type among the three, as this give the user the ability to have one of the many types of superman abilities. It is the broadest type as they are anything other than having the ability to have any animal like abilities or have similarities to certain types of elements, since those types are already for the Zoan Devil Fruit and the Logia Devil Fruit. A few types of Paramecia fruits can ive people the ability to temporarily or permanently change their physical appearance on their body or themselves as a whole. Some of the powers are usually from the user generating their power from nothing, while others visually show based on appearance. While it is common for the Devil Fruit power to be seen physically, other Paramecia Devil Fruit powers alter other internally and gives the ability to influence their surrounding environment on both objects and living things. Many of those people can change anything or someone around them merely by touching it, but others can create a specific substance that is used to achieve the desired effect.

Zoan
Zoan Devil Fruits are the animal type fruit that gives the user the ability to change into a certain animal, all while still combining the characteristic of both the animal and the users own species which is most typically human. Zoan Devil Fruits that are carnivore are particularly good for physical combats because of their predatory features such as claws, fangs, durability, and size good for fighting. Zoan powers can significantly improve on the users physical ability, but this is also dependent on the type, whether it is temporary or permanent physical feature that they can manually have in use or always have it shown. Certain Zoan Devil Fruit provides skills based on the type of species, an example can be a bird like capability that lets them fly, a reptile power that allows the user to spit venom, or a burrowing Zoan that makes the user quickly dig through anything. There are also some sub categories for the Zoan Devil Fruit, being Ancient which makes the user transform into an extinct species such as dinosaurs, Mythical which allows the user to transform into a mythological creature such as a dragon or phoenix, or artificial which gives the user to take on any animal characteristic either permanently or at will.

Logia
Logia Devil Fruits are the most rarest and are considered the most powerful of the three Devil Fruit types in the One Piece universe. As these fruits are able to grant the power to create, control, and transform ones body into an element of nature such as fire, ice, lightning, sand, mud, magma, smoke, soot snow, gas, plants, light, and darkness. If the user transforms into their element at will, they are able to take form in their element and be separated into pieces based on what form they are, but, the smaller pieces can’t be as controlled compared to being one whole. Additionally, a Logia user can control things logically associated with their element, such as sand draining moisture, light traveling through the highest speed, gas controlling the atmosphere, or darkness neutralizing other Devil Fruit abilities that are in contact. Their main advantage is intangibility, which makes them nearly immune to harm from non-Haki users by enabling users to let physical attacks pass through them. Logia users are not invincible, despite their great power. Haki is often used to get around this physicality in the "New World" section of the series. Some believe that Mythical Zoan fruits may become equally or more powerful after a certain degree of Haki knowledge, even though they are still regarded as superior to many other fruits.
